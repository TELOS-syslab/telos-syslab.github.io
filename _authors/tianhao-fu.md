Tianhao Fu is currently pursuing the Ph.D. degree in computer science at Peking University, Beijing, China, supervised by Professors Yingwei Luo and Xiaolin Wang. His research interests include optimizing resource scheduling in graphics processing unit (GPU) clusters, with particular emphasis on large model training and inference, as well as the development of GPU cluster scheduling simulators.
