TELOS members attended **OSDI 2026** in Seattle, USA. During the conference, Kang Hu presented **SBB: Eliminating Centralized Bottlenecks in Userspace Network Runtime**, and Liujia Li presented **Merlin: An Efficient Adaptive Cache Eviction Algorithm via Fine-Grained Characterization**.
